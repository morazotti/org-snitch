** DONE 20260311170708 Integrating with Magit
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       098ea33bb34f0ca3f4c2016f556bd609
:TASK_NUM: 5
:END:

To enhance your workflow with `org-snitch`, you can integrate it with `magit` to streamline project notes and task management directly from your version control interface.

1. **Linking Commits to Tasks:** Automatically generate links to relevant project tasks when making commits. This can improve traceability and context for future code reviews.

2. **Inserting Task References in Commit Messages:**
   You can insert links to specific tasks directly into your commit messages using `org-snitch-insert-link` before you finish writing your commit.

3. **Tracking Task Progress Through Commits:**
   Flesh out your work by referencing tasks in your commits. Use `org-snitch-find-references` to search for these task references across your commits, providing an audit trail of task progression within your project’s version history.

By combining `org-snitch` and `magit`, you create a seamless bridge between your project's version control and task documentation, making it easier to maintain clear communication and organization within your development workflow.
